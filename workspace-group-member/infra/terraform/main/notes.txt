Workspace Group Member Module - Notes
======================================

This module assigns users and service principals as members of Databricks groups.

Two assignment modes:
  1. Name-based: provide group_names + user_names + membership_assignments
     (uses data sources to resolve IDs automatically)
  2. Direct ID-based: provide direct_members with explicit group_id + member_id

Provider requirement:
  - Workspace-level Databricks provider

Prerequisites:
  - Groups must exist (created by workspace-group module)
  - Users/SPs must exist in the workspace

Related modules:
  - workspace-group: creates the groups
  - workspace-role-assignment: assigns groups to workspace (USER/ADMIN)
  - workspace-entitlements: manages workspace capabilities per group
  - workspace-permissions: manages object-level permissions per group
